Fettucine Alfredo


This easy pasta recipe is perfect when you are craving a hearty plate of pasta. Done in 20 minutes, it is one of my go to recipes.


Ingredients

            
  • 500g        Fettucine pasta
    • 
        
  • 50g         Butter
    • 
        
  • 3              Large garlic cloves
    • 
        
  • 400ml       Heavy cream
    • 
        
  • 300g        Parmesan cheese
    • 
        
  • 1 tsp       Salt
    • 
        
  • 1/2 tsp     Black pepper
    •

Directions

            
  1. In a large pot, salt some water and bring it to a boil. Add the pasta once it's boiling and cook as directed on the package.
    2. 
        
  2. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a large skillet, mince the garlic and add it in.
    3. 
        
  3. After 2 minutes, add the heavy cream, salt and pepper. Let it heat up until it bubbles.
    4. 
        
  4. Grate the parmesan cheese. Remove the sauce from the heat and add the cheese.
    5. 
        
  5. Add the pasta to the sauce and add pasta water if needed, to adjust the consistency of the sauce.
    6. 
        
  6. Serve with extra grated parmesan on top and optional parsley.
    7.
